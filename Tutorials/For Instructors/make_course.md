## Tutorial
To create a course, click the **Create Course** button at the top-right corner of your instructor courses table. 

<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 5 30 01 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7b50fa0a-f48e-450d-8b53-c20035f8ad49" />

A modal will appear prompting you to enter the course name, term, and school. Fill out the form and submit it to create your course.
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 5 34 04 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/10751cdf-8429-47ef-b3bb-6a4c540453b6" />

After submitting, the new course will appear in your instructor courses table. You can update the course name, term, or school using the **edit** button.
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 5 34 57 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/aef4fa65-e36d-4bfc-900b-418740f45405" />

After creating a course, you can link a GitHub organization to your course by clicking the **Install GitHub App** button in that course’s row.
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 5 35 29 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f1b919d7-19c2-4196-919d-178ab8061407" />

After clicking the button, you'll be prompted to choose an organization you want to link. 
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 5 38 37 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/930afe8e-2f10-4057-808f-b799e578ef68" />

Once the organization is linked, the **Install GitHub App** button will bew ill be replaced with a link to the GitHub organization and a Github settings icon for managing the connection between your course and GitHub.
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 5 39 25 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/43e68374-5502-490b-8efa-d088d6909af5" />

To uninstall the Frontiers connection from the GitHub organization, click the GitHub settings icon. You will be redireted to GitHub.
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 5 40 17 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/70377c83-66aa-4320-a6a7-194c8cf6a4e9" />

Then, on GitHub, scroll to the bottom of the page and click the **Uninstall**. 
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 5 42 05 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a8ba4f6f-242b-41ac-8f2e-ccdb74b6a473" />
