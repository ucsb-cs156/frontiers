## Tutorial
To login, click the login button at the top-right of the screen.
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-18 at 4 39 19 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/04a93450-4084-48b9-86af-135536c8501e" />

Next, choose the credentials you want use to sign in to Frontiers. 
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-18 at 4 39 35 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4a9c6d29-8aff-4eae-aa22-35e35f37eb0c" />

If this is your first time logging in, you'll be promted to sign in with GitHub. You won't be able to continue using Frontiers' features until your GitHub account is linked.
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="516004860-f4c47c8d-5841-4990-94fa-b42dcb2ae609" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/19f6abe0-67bb-49ae-b97e-2ca8d28a7f49" />

Once you've signed in and connected your GitHub, you'll be taken to the homepage. As an instructor, you should see a table displaying your current courses on Frontiers, along with a button to add a course. 
<img width="1125" height="600" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-19 at 5 30 01 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/40e2e738-1e5d-4648-97ff-9ba90edd4a1a" />
