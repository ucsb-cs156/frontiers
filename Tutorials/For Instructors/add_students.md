## Tutorial
Frontiers allows you to add students individually or through a roster upload. To access the **Students** page, click the course name in your insturctor courses table, then navigate to the **Students** tab. 


To add an individual student, click **Add Individual Student**. A modal will appear prompting you to enter the Student Id, first name, last name, and email. Fill out the form and submit it to add the student. You can update all of the studentâ€™s information except their email using the **edit** button.

To add students using a roster, click **Upload CSV Roster** and upload your CSV file. For details on the required CSV format, open the  **Help** dropdown in the top-right corner next to the **Log Out** button and select **CSV Upload/Download Formats**.
